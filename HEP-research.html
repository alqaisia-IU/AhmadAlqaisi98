High Energy Physics Research

My current research focuses on experimental neutrino physics and the DUNE collaboration.

DUNE (Deep Underground Neutrino Experiment)

As a PhD student at Indiana University, I am a member of the DUNE collaboration. This experiment aims to study neutrino oscillations and search for proton decay.

  • Collaboration: Working under Prof. Jon Urheim.
  • Objective: Enhancing energy resolution using scintillation light.

ProtoDUNE Vertical Drift (VD)

My primary focus is on the Photon Detection System (PDS) for the ProtoDUNE Vertical Drift detector at CERN.

  • Hardware: Testing X-ARAPUCA photon collectors.
  • Analysis: Optimizing reconstruction of neutrino events in LAr.
